## 0. Core Thesis

My AI investment thesis is not simply “AI will grow.” The more investable thesis is:

> As AI applications move from demos to production workflows, token consumption and inference workloads should grow materially. That should increase demand for compute, custom silicon, networking, memory, storage, cloud capacity, data centers, power, cooling, and grid infrastructure.

The investment chain:

```text
AI application adoption
→ token / inference demand
→ GPU / ASIC / accelerator demand
→ high-bandwidth memory + networking demand
→ hyperscaler cloud capacity expansion
→ data center construction
→ power, cooling, electrical equipment, grid, and generation demand
```

This watch list is designed around that chain.

## 5. Suggested Quant Research Framework

### 5.1 Factor Groups

Use these factor groups to screen the universe.

#### Momentum

| Factor | Description |
|---|---|
| 3M price momentum | Captures near-term trend |
| 6M price momentum | Captures medium-term trend |
| 12M price momentum excluding last month | Classic momentum factor |
| Relative strength vs QQQM | Identifies AI names outperforming large-cap tech |
| Relative strength vs SMH | Identifies winners within AI/semi complex |

#### Quality

| Factor | Description |
|---|---|
| Gross margin level | Pricing power / product quality |
| Gross margin trend | Competitive pressure indicator |
| Operating margin | Profit conversion |
| Free cash flow margin | Cash generation |
| ROIC / ROE | Capital efficiency |
| Net debt / EBITDA | Balance sheet risk |

#### Growth

| Factor | Description |
|---|---|
| Revenue growth YoY | Demand growth |
| Revenue acceleration | Improving trend |
| EPS growth | Earnings leverage |
| Forward revenue revisions | Analyst expectation change |
| Forward EPS revisions | Earnings estimate momentum |

#### Valuation

| Factor | Description |
|---|---|
| Forward P/E | Earnings multiple |
| EV/Sales | Useful for high-growth names |
| EV/EBITDA | Cross-sector comparison |
| FCF yield | Cash-flow valuation |
| PEG ratio | Growth-adjusted valuation |

#### AI Exposure

This is a semi-manual score from 1 to 5.

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 5 | Direct AI infrastructure revenue driver |
| 4 | High AI exposure, but mixed business |
| 3 | Meaningful AI adjacency |
| 2 | Indirect AI exposure |
| 1 | Weak AI linkage |

Examples:
- NVDA: 5
- AVGO: 5
- VRT: 5
- MSFT: 4
- GOOGL: 4
- CRM: 3
- NEE: 2–3 depending on data center power contracts
